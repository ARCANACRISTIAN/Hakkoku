French and Japanese restaurant

The best option for a meal when visiting one of the prettiest villages of Alsace, in the northern Vosges. Among the dishes on the menu are a feuilleté of local Munster cheese and choucroute with fish.

Pulsating with energy and dripping with Michelin stars,Hakkoku is as nondescript as it gets; after all, with six seats at the counter in the main dining room, plus two more rooms with counters manned by the chef's apprentices, the main feature is the wooden sushi bar itself.
